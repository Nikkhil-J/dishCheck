About This Dish

A classic Hyderabadi-style dum biryani with fragrant basmati rice layered with tender marinated chicken, slow-cooked over charcoal. The meat falls off the bone, infused with saffron, fried onions, and a blend of aromatic spices. Served with raita and mirchi ka salan on the side. The portion is generous — easily enough for two.
